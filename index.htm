THE INTOUCHABLES

Duration: 1h52m | 2011 | R | IMDb rating: 8.5/10

After he becomes a quadriplegic from a paragliding accident, an aristocrat hires a young man from the projects to be his caregiver.

The Intouchables is a 2011 French buddy comedy-drama film. This heartfelt film follows the unlikely friendship between Philippe, a wealthy man living with quadriplegia, and Driss, a young man from a tough background who becomes his live-in caregiver. Coming from completely different worlds, the two clash at first—but Driss’s bold, unconventional approach soon brings new energy and humor into Philippe’s carefully controlled life. As they spend time together, both men are changed by the relationship: Philippe is pushed to reconnect with joy, risk, and human connection, while Driss discovers new perspectives, responsibilities, and opportunities he never imagined. Blending humor, warmth, and emotion, the film explores dignity, trust, and the power of friendship to transform lives beyond social boundaries.
